My Apps

For my final project my partner and I created a Blackjack program. If you do not know how to play Blackjack I will briefly explain the rules. The goal of the game is to have a total of 21 or as close to 21 as possible with the cards you have. Initially you are given 2 cards and the dealer has 1 card visible. When you click the hit button it will give you another card. When you are satisfied with your total you click the stand button. The stand button will lock in your cards and calculate the total of both your cards and the dealer's cards. Depending on who wins it will pop up with a message corresponding to that program.
